## Graphic designer
**Graphic-design** is the art, profession, and practice of creating visual content 
   to communicate messages, ideas, and information effectively

### Why Students Should Study It
- To Be Able Builds Brand Identity & Trust.
- To Boosts Employability & Freelancing.
- To Be Able To Develop Problem-Solving Skills.
- To Learn Effective Communication Through Graphics.
- To Creative Expression & Career Opportunities

### How to Get Started
1. One Can Get Free Online Courses on Youtube.
2. For simple projects,Canva is excellent forsocial media graphics, & learning the basics.
3. Instal Adobe Illustrator is best for vector graphics (logos, illustration),
    while Photoshop is best for photo editing.
